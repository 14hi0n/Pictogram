  <h1>Pictogram</h1>
  <p>Browser extension for sharing anime art to Telegram channels</p>
</div>

---

> ⚠️ **Alpha status** - actively developed. Expect bugs, breaking changes, and missing features.


Share media from anime websites directly to Telegram using a bot - just one click, no copying and pasting. It automatically extracts hashtags, links, and the author's name.

---

## Supported Browser
-   Google Chrome
-   Mises (Android)
-   Orion (IOS) [\*](#orion-note)

## Supported Websites

-   Danbooru
-   Pixiv
-   ZeroChan
